Pizza

homemade pizza

Learn how to make an exquisite homemade pizza for your guests and surpise them!

Ingredients

  • 3 or 4 eggs
  • 200gr self-rising flour
  • 300gr Mozzarela
  • 200gr ham
  • 5 tomatoes
  • salt
  • oregano
  • basil
  • sugar
  • olive oil
  • *optional* olives

Steps

  1. First we are gonna mix well the eggs and the flour, put salt to taste. Let the mass rest for 20 minutes
  2. while we wait, let's prepare the sauce. In a pan with olive oil put the tomatose without skin.
  3. After a 3 or 4 minutes, add seasonings to taste and mix for one minute. Now you should have ready your tomatoes sauce
  4. If our mass is ready let's roll it out. You can give it the form of a square or a circle
  5. when we finish, put de sauce on the mass.
  6. Put the ham and mozzarella in slices
  7. now we have our pizza ready, cook in oven for 15 minutes ant enjoy!
